    🛡️ Prevention Guides

    Small behavioral changes save countless lives. Knowing what to do makes a difference.

    For Drivers

    • Respect Speed Limits: Maintain 30-40km/h in forest zones.
    • Night Driving: Avoid high-beams; animals freeze like headlights deer.
    • No Honking: Loud noises disorient wildlife.

    For the Public

    • Don't Crowd: If you see an injured animal, give it space.
    • Do NOT treat: Wild animals carry diseases. Call the authorities.
    • Document Safely: Note location and take a photo from afar.
